Cards

Amity loyalty cards & QR codes

This page collects speculative Amity loyalty cards: QR codes and barcodes that imagine how a shared customer identity might move through Tesco-style systems. For the project, these cards are artefacts to be scanned in exhibitions or documentation, not live accounts at tills.

Each card can link back to this website, a project PDF, or a specific narrative about how Amity behaves within one store’s ecosystem.
